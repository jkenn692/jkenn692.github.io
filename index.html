Freshman in Greenville, South Carolina

I am a freshman at Bob Jones University majoring in Studio Arts. art has always been a part of my life. I tend to work in many different media of art. One media I have worked on for a majority of my life, is comic illustrating.

For years I have developed and illustraded my character called Merlin and his friends.

I have found using art to communicate my everyday life through comics is much like keeping a diary. Creating Merlin and Friends has always been my way to bring others happiness
