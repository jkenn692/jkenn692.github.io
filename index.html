Hello there!

My name is Jessica Kennedy! I am a student at Bob Jones University majoring in Studio Arts. Art has always been a part of my life. I work in many different mediums from sculpting to animation! Using my art to communicate my everyday life is a way of keeping a diary. 

For years I have developed and illustraded my character called Merlin. He is the heart of what i want to achieve with my art. Creating 'Merlin and Friends' has always been my way of bringing others happiness and laughs.
